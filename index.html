How to Play

Welcome to the 2048 Game! Here's how to play:

  • Use arrow keys (, , , ) or swipe gestures (, , , ) to move tiles.
  • Click the "Reshuffle Board" button to shuffle the existing tiles on the board.
  • Merge tiles with the same number to create a new tile with a doubled value.
  • Your goal is to reach the 2048 tile!
  • The game ends when there are no more valid moves or when you reach 2048.

Have fun playing!
